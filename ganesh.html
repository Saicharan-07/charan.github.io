RRR MOVIE

RRR (which stands for Rise, Roar, Revolt) is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language period action drama movie.
It is written and directed by S. S. Rajamouli. The film is a fictional story about two Indian revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju (Charan) and Komaram Bheem (Rama Rao),
who fought against the British Raj and Nizam of Hyderabad respectively.
It stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, Olivia Morris, and Ajay Devgn.
